EagleEye does not try to compete with other photo applications on the market since, for instance, it's not an application where users can actively organize their photos. Instead, EagleEye provides a different way for users to enjoy their photos by giving them the ability to look at the collection as a whole.
Applications like Picasa or iPhoto don't allow a user to take a step back and look at the whole collection. iPhoto allows an organization by events but there's still the need to scroll through all the events.
EagleEye takes a different approach and displays all images on the screen at the same time. This allows immediate recognition of some groupings by the groups o colors. But since this makes images to small to be understandable, EagleEye allows the user to smoothly zoom in on the canvas and take a closer look at the photos.
